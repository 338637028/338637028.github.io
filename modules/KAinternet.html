Khan Academy AP®︎/College Computer Science Principles: Computing Innovations & The Internet

This module introduces both the social factors that dictate the internet and the computer innovations that hold the world wide web afloat. Students were tasked with learning specifically about web protocols, internet infastructure, digital divide and methods of communication among internet users. The content of this module can contribute to future education as the content taught can be applied in other fields of learning that relate to social institutions and complex systems. The most impactful part of this module was the introduction of the backend of the internet and the basics of how protocols work.
