2022 Portfolio Modules

Creative Computation Art Show

This module served as a preperation for the first larger scale project, the Hackathon. Students were tasked with making an object-oriented visual sketch that contained multiple scenes, all using the p5.js library. This module gave experience of completing work in a short time span and application of entire libraries, two skills that can benefit students in their future educational career. The most impactful element of this module was the application of coding outside of a very controlled environment such as the Khan Academy or codecademy editors.
